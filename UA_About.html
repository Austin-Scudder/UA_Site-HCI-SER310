Welcome to UA High

U.A. High is Japan’s most prestigious school for Heroes and hero related industry. U.A. strives for excellence in both the classroom and the daily lives of all its students, building the next generation of heroes to protect your community.With a large campus hosting a multitude varying training grounds and learning centers, as well as the staff comprising of Professional heroes with years of experience under their belts, students of any quirk can freely train and learn without fear of loss of control or damages either to themselves others, nor any terrain.

Our Staff All of U.A.’s staff are the top of the line best of the best heroes and professional staff. We have teachers such as The R-Rated Hero Midnight, The Media Hero Present Mike, and of course our newest teacher, the Symbol of Peace himself, All Might. With such a reputable line of staff behind the education and well being of every student as well as our state of the art security system where no outside person can infiltrate the premises. You can be sure that every student is 100% safe and sound within these walls of UA Academy.

Our Goal and Motto

Within UA, we believe there is no problem too small, or person too far gone for our heroes. There is no limit to our heroism and devotion to protecting and serving those around us. We devote ourselves to never give up, never surrender, and to always go beyond our limits; PLUS ULTRA!
