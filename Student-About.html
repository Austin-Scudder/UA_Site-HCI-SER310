Izuku Midoriya is the main protagonist of My Hero Academia. He was born without any powers in a world were 80 percent of humans have abilities known as Quirks, but that didn't stop him from trying to be a hero. He is later given a quirk by his idol and the number 1 hero, All Might after proving himself as a his successor. After enrolling in a prestigious super hero school, he embarks on a journey to become a powerful hero and symbol of peace.

Katsuki is a student at UA attending in the hopes of becoming a professional hero. His Quirk, or power, uses the sweat from his palms to create explosions. He is very hot-headed and believes that he can be the best hero ever. He also strongly dislikes Izuku.

Ochako Uraraka (a.k.a. Uravity) is a hero in training, and Izuku Midoriya's closest friend and crush. With her gravity-based powers, she plans to be a hero in order to provide her parents with an easier life.
